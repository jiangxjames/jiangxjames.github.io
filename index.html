Brief Biography

I am a graduating Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA, under the supervision of Prof. Lieven Vandenberghe. I hold an M.S. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA. Prior to that, I received Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Hong Kong.

Research Interests

My research interests broadly include theory and algorithms for convex optimization, especially large-scale semidefinite programming. I design, analyze, and implement efficient and scalable algorithms for various applications, including signal processing, machine learning, and graph-related problems.
