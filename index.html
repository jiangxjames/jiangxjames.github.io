Brief Biography

I am now a postdoctoral researcher hosted by Program in the Foundations and Applications of Mathematical Optimization and Data Science (MODS) at Lehigh University. In 2022, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at UCLA, under the supervision of Prof. Lieven Vandenberghe. Prior to that, I received Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from the University of Hong Kong.

Research Interests

My research interests broadly include theory and algorithms for optimization, especially large-scale semidefinite programming. I design, analyze, and implement efficient and scalable algorithms for various applications including engineering, machine learning and data science.
